This data was collected through an interactive on-line version of the Generic Conspiracist Beliefs Scale in 2016.
See Brotherton, Robert, Christopher C. French, and Alan D. Pickering. "Measuring belief in conspiracy theories: the generic conspiracist beliefs scale." Frontiers in psychology 4 (2013). 
Visitors completed the test primarily for personal amusement.
At the end of the test but before the results were displayed, users were asked if they would be willing to complete an additional survey and allow their responses to be saved for research. Only users who agreed yes are in this dataset. Individuals with age < 13 were not recorded.

Constructs / Factors
1) Government Malfeasance (GM)
2) Extraterrestrial Cover-up (ET)
3) Malevolent Global Conspiracies (MG)
4) Personal Wellbeing (PW)
5) Control of Information (CI)

The responses to the GCBS are in (question numbers match to items in TABLE A1 of Brotherton, et. al. 2013.):

Q1 - Q15

Q1 (GM) The government is involved in the murder of innocent citizens and/or well-known public figures, and keeps this a secret
Q6 (GM) The government permits or perpetrates acts of terrorism on its own soil, disguising its involvement 
Q11 (GM) The government uses people as patsies to hide its involvement in criminal activity
Q2 (MG) The power held by heads of state is second to the of small unknown groups who really control world politics
Q7 (MG) A small, secret group of people is responsible for making all major world decisions, such as going to war
Q12 (MG) Certain significant events have been the result of the activity of a small group who secretly manipulate world events
Q3 (ET) Secret organizations communicate with extraterrestrials, but keep this fact from the public
Q8 (ET) Evidence of alien contact is being concealed from the public
Q13 (ET) Some UFO sightings and rumors are planned or staged in order to distract the public from real alien contact
Q4 (PW) The spread of certain viruses and/or diseases is the result of the deliberate, concealed efforts of some organization
Q9 (PW) Technology with mind-control capacities is used on people without their knowledge
Q14 (PW) Experiments involving new drugs or technologies are routinely carried out on the public without their knowledge or consent
Q5 (CI) Groups of scientists manipulate, fabricate, or suppress evidence in order to deceive the public
Q10 (CI) New and advanced technology which would harm current industry is being suppressed
Q15 (CI) A lot of important information is deliberately concealed from the public out of self-interest

The Ten Item Personality Inventory was administered (see Gosling, S. D., Rentfrow, P. J., & Swann, W. B., Jr. (2003). A Very Brief Measure of the Big Five Personality Domains. Journal of Research in Personality, 37, 504-528.):

TIPI1	Extraverted, enthusiastic.
TIPI2	Critical, quarrelsome.
TIPI3	Dependable, self-disciplined.
TIPI4	Anxious, easily upset.
TIPI5	Open to new experiences, complex.
TIPI6	Reserved, quiet.
TIPI7	Sympathetic, warm.
TIPI8	Disorganized, careless.
TIPI9	Calm, emotionally stable.
TIPI10	Conventional, uncreative.

The TIPI items were rated "I see myself as:" _____ such that

1 = Disagree strongly
2 = Disagree moderately
3 = Disagree a little
4 = Neither agree nor disagree
5 = Agree a little
6 = Agree moderately
7 = Agree strongly
